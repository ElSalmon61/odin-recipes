No-Bean Chili Recipe

Description

The No-Bean Chili is perfect for people who don't like
the spiciness of traditional chili, who don't digest
beans very well or simply for people that like chili,
but want to put a different spin on it.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon bacon grease
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef, preferably chuck
  • ½ medium white onion, minced
  • 8 cloves garlic, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomato puree
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 cube beef bouillon

Steps

  1. Heat bacon grease in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in
    the hot pot until almost browned, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to medium and add
    onion; cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion has softened and turned
    translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, chili powder, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper; cook and stir until
    fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes. Add tomato puree, water, tomato paste, sugar, and bouillon
    cube. Reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 to 60
    minutes.
